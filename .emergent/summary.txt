*   **Stock & Customers Pages**:
    *   Filterable lists of products and customers.

    *   Users must first select filters (like date range) and then generate the report.
    *   Ability to export reports to PDF or Excel (Future Task).
*   **General & UI/UX**:
    *   The app must have a highly polished design with Light/Dark themes.
